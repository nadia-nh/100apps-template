# 100 App Ideas

## 1. Pomodoro Flow
A focus timer with ambient nature sounds that automatically adjust volume based on session phase. During work sessions it plays subtle forest rain, shifting to gentle birdsong during breaks. Visual breathing guide pulses on screen during transitions.

## 2. Water Log
A hydration tracker with a growing animated water tank that fills as you log drinks. Shows daily progress as percentage and compares to your custom goal. Gentle notifications remind you to drink.

## 3. Split the Bill
Enter total bill and number of people, optionally add custom amounts per person for items they ordered specifically. Shows tip options (15%, 18%, 20%, custom) and splits everything evenly or by item.

## 4. Habit Circles
A minimalist habit tracker showing the current week as seven circles. Tap to fill a circle when you complete a habit. Long press to see a 30-day streak visualization. Multiple habits displayed in a grid.

## 5. Flash Deck
Create flashcard decks with question on front, answer on back. Swipe right if you knew it, left if you didn't. Spaced repetition algorithm schedules cards based on performance. Add new cards with a simple form.

## 6. Color Mood
Select colors that match your current mood from a curated palette. Logs your mood entries with timestamp. Shows a monthly calendar heat map of your emotional patterns. Exportable as a simple data summary.

## 7. Chore Rotate
Add household chores and household members. Automatically rotates assignments each week with a single click. Shows current week's assignments and a history of who did what. Visual checklist for completion.

## 8. Plant Parent
Track watering schedules for each plant with custom intervals. Photos of each plant (stored as base64). Next watering date shown prominently with overdue alerts. Quick "watered" button logs the action.

## 9. Reading Log
Track books you're reading, want to read, and have finished. Shows page progress bar for current book. Logs date started and finished with star rating. Simple reading speed calculation based on pages and time.

## 10. UUID Gen
Generate UUIDs with one click. Choose format: standard UUID v4, short 8-character ID, or nanoid-style. Copy to clipboard button. History of last 20 generated IDs visible.

## 11. Time Block
Visual weekly calendar where you drag to create time blocks. Each block has a color category (work, exercise, rest, etc.). Shows total hours per category. Simple start/end time adjustment.

## 12. Coffee Brew
Select bean type, roast level, and brew method (pour over, French press, espresso). Shows recommended ratio, water temperature, and brew time. Timer with distinct phases for each method. Notes field for adjustments.

## 13. Body Timer
Set a 20-20-20 reminder: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. Displays countdown timer with progress ring. Tracks daily eye rest compliance percentage.

## 14. Compliment Bot
Generate a random genuine compliment with one tap. Categories: friendly, professional, playful, heartfelt. Copy to clipboard. Rate compliments to train preferences. History of generated compliments.

## 15. Budget Envelope
Create budget categories (groceries, entertainment, etc.) with monthly amounts. Log expenses by category. Visual "envelope" shows remaining budget. Warning when approaching limit. Monthly reset option.

## 16. Phrase Collector
Save useful phrases in different languages with pronunciation guides. Categorize by situation (travel, business, emergency). Searchable list. Tap to hear text-to-speech pronunciation.

## 17. Sleep Clock
Record bedtime and wake time with simple start/stop buttons. Shows sleep duration and consistency score. Weekly average display. Optional notes field for sleep quality.

## 18. Posture Check
Random interval reminders to check posture (configurable 15-60 min). Quick tap: "good" or "bad" posture. Daily posture score percentage. Weekly trend chart.

## 19. Tip Quick
Enter bill amount, select tip percentage, and see the tip amount and total. Custom split counter. Remembers last used percentage. History of recent calculations.

## 20. Coin Flip
Large animated coin that flips when tapped. Choose between 1-10 coins. Tracks flip history (heads/tails count). Perfect for making quick decisions or games.

## 21. Dice Roll
Roll 1-6 dice with custom sides (4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 20). Animated roll with physics feel. Shows sum of all dice. History of last 10 rolls. Save custom dice combinations.

## 22. Picker Wheel
Create a custom spinner with your own options (names, choices, numbers). Spin animation with sound. Weighted options support. Save multiple wheels for different occasions.

## 23. Would You Rather
Get random "would you rather" questions to spark conversations. Swipe to skip, tap for new question. Save favorite questions. Categories: fun, deep, silly, serious.

## 24. Story Seed
Generate random story prompts: character, setting, conflict, and object. Combines elements into a creative writing prompt. Tap to regenerate any element. Save prompts you like.

## 25. Recipe Cost
Enter recipe ingredients with price and quantity. Calculate total cost and cost per serving. Compare recipes by cost efficiency. Store recipes with ingredient lists.

## 26. Pantry Check
Track pantry items with quantities and expiration dates. Sort by expiry to see what to use first. Quick "use" button decrements quantity. Low stock alerts for items you frequently buy.

## 27. Currency Snap
Quick currency converter with popular conversions. Type amount in one field, see conversion in others. Works offline with cached rates. Last updated timestamp shown.

## 28. World Clock
Display multiple time zones as analog or digital clocks. World map with dots showing locations. Time difference calculator between any two zones. DST awareness.

## 29. Packing List
Create reusable packing templates (weekend trip, beach vacation, business). Check off items as you pack. Add custom items to any list. Templates stored for reuse.

## 30. Bucket List
Track goals and experiences you want to have. Mark as dream, in progress, or completed. Add location tags and estimated cost. Show completion percentage by category.

## 31. Quote Vault
Save inspirational quotes with author and source. Tag by theme (motivation, love, humor). Random quote display. Searchable collection. Copy quote to clipboard.

## 32. Sound Board
Play ambient sounds and effects. Mix multiple sounds with individual volume sliders. Save custom mixes as presets. Categories: nature, urban, white noise, retro.

## 33. Palette Pick
Generate random color palettes (5 colors). Lock colors you like and regenerate the rest. Copy any color as hex, RGB, or HSL. Save palettes you create.

## 34. Hash It
Generate hashes (MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256) from text input. One-click copy any hash. Compare two hashes for equality. Common use: verify file integrity.

## 35. JSON Pretty
Paste or type JSON and see it pretty-printed with syntax highlighting. Minify option. Validate JSON and show errors with line numbers. Copy formatted output.

## 36. Regex Test
Test regular expressions against sample text in real-time. Shows all matches highlighted. Displays capture groups. Common pattern library to insert.

## 37. Cron Read
Parse cron expressions into human-readable schedules. Build cron expressions with dropdowns. Shows next 5 run times. Handles standard 5-field cron format.

## 38. Base Encode
Encode text to Base64 or decode Base64 to text. URL-safe Base64 option. Handles binary data representation. Copy result to clipboard.

## 39. Unit Convert
Convert between units in various categories: length, weight, temperature, data, time. Shows all conversions for a value at once. Swap units with one tap.

## 40. Icebreaker
Generate conversation starter questions for meetings or dates. Categories: get to know you, fun, deep, team building. Tap to refresh. Save favorites for later.

## 41. Toast Maker
Generate ready-to-use toasts for birthdays, anniversaries, weddings. Customize with names. Multiple tone options (funny, heartfelt, formal). Copy to clipboard.

## 42. Decision Dump
Enter multiple options for a decision. Randomly select one with animation. Weighted selection option (some choices more likely). History of past decisions.

## 43. Secret Santa
Enter participant names and generate random assignments. Constraints support (can't pair with who). Email copy of assignment (via mailto link). Re-roll option.

## 44. Group Spend
Track group expenses over time. Split equally or custom amounts per person. Who owes whom summary at end. Export summary as text.

## 45. Gift Idea
Store gift ideas for people with budget range. Mark as purchased. Notes on preferences they've mentioned. Sort by occasion and budget.

## 46. Medication Mind
Set medication schedules with frequency and times. Logging buttons for each dose. Adherence percentage tracking. Refill reminders based on remaining dose count.

## 47. Breathe Easy
Guided breathing exercises with visual expanding/contracting circle. Multiple patterns: 4-7-8, box breathing, energizing. Session duration selector. Haptic feedback option.

## 48. Word Collector
Save interesting words you encounter. Add definition, pronunciation, example sentence. Tag by language or topic. Random word review feature. Export word list.

## 49. Card Sort
Sort items by dragging into ranked order. Perfect for prioritizing tasks, top 10 lists, or any ordered ranking. Save and compare different sort sessions.

## 50. Daily Rank
Rank your top 3 priorities for today each morning. Shows your rankings from past days. Reflect on whether you completed them. Weekly summary view.

## 51. Focus Score
At end of day, rate your focus/productivity 1-10. Add a brief note about what impacted it. Weekly focus trend chart. Correlation insights between notes and scores.

## 52. Energy Map
Track energy levels throughout the day (morning, afternoon, evening). Note activities that boosted or drained energy. Identify patterns in what energizes you.

## 53. Skill Tree
Visualize skills you want to develop with milestones. Mark skills as learning, practiced, mastered. Time spent on each skill. Progress visualization.

## 54. Note Stack
Quick capture for fleeting thoughts. Stack view shows most recent at top. Swipe to archive or delete. Search through past notes. Timestamp on each note.

## 55. Meeting Prep
Agenda builder for meetings. Add topics with time allocations. Shows elapsed time during meeting. Notes field per topic. Summary generator.

## 56. Quick Poll
Create instant polls for group decisions. Options for yes/no, multiple choice, or scale. Real-time vote counting. Share results as image.

## 57. Countdown
Create countdowns to events with custom names and dates. Shows days, hours, minutes remaining. Notification on event day. Multiple countdowns view.

## 58. Uptime Clock
Display how long until next significant time point (end of work day, weekend, month). Updates in real-time. Multiple simultaneous countdowns.

## 59. Stand Counter
Track standing desk usage. Start/stop timer for each standing session. Daily and weekly totals. Goal setting for standing time.

## 60. Gratitude Log
Record three things you're grateful for each day. Entries dated and searchable. Periodic review of past gratitudes. Tags for categorization.

## 61. Thought Catch
Quick capture for ideas or worries. Categorize as idea, worry, task, or note. Review mode shows thoughts to process. Mark as resolved or keep.

## 62. Meal Plan
Weekly meal planner with drag-and-drop. Auto-generate shopping list from plan. Save favorite meals. Rotate meals to reduce decision fatigue.

## 63. Calorie Rough
Quick calorie estimator from selecting food items and portions. No precise tracking, just awareness. Daily total with simple goals. Common foods quick-add.

## 64. Receipt Hold
Capture receipt photos with date and store name. Tag with category. Monthly expense summary by category. Search by date or store.

## 65. Goal Path
Visual goal tracker with milestones. Add goal with target date and milestones. Progress percentage display. Notes on each milestone completion.

## 66. Distance Log
Track workouts by type (walk, run, bike, swim). Enter distance and duration. Auto-calculate pace. Weekly mileage summaries. Personal records highlighted.

## 67. Weight Track
Log weight entries with date. Shows trend line over time. Goal weight with progress. Moving average display to smooth fluctuations.

## 68. Rep Counter
Simple rep counter for exercises. Large tap target for counting during workout. Rest timer between sets. Save workout summaries.

## 69. Mood Waves
Track mood multiple times per day. Shows mood graph over time. Weather correlation (optional). Notes field for context.

## 70. Social Battery
Rate your social energy level 1-10 throughout the day. Activity logging to find draining/charging patterns. Predict optimal times for social activities.

## 71. Screen Time
Manual log of screen time activities. Categories: work, social media, entertainment, reading. Daily totals with warnings over limits. Weekly trends.

## 72. Traffic Mind
Estimate commute times at different departure times. Log actual vs estimated. Build personal model of best/worst travel times.

## 73. Pack Track
Track packages by carrier and tracking number. Status display (ordered, shipped, out for delivery, delivered). Manual refresh option.

## 74. Appliance Age
Record purchase date and warranty expiration for appliances. Shows age in years/months. Warranty alert before expiry. Estimated replacement cost.

## 75. Light Stock
Inventory of light bulbs by type and location. Track quantity on hand. Warn when running low. Link to recent purchases.

## 76. House Fix
Log home maintenance tasks with frequency. Shows upcoming maintenance. Mark complete with date. Cost tracking for repairs.

## 77. Car Care
Track vehicle maintenance: oil changes, tire rotations, inspections. Mileage-based reminders. Service history log. Cost tracking.

## 78. Crypto Watch
Track cryptocurrency prices with manual refresh. Favorite coins list. Price change percentages. No real trading, just watching.

## 79. Sub Count
Track monthly subscription services. Shows total monthly/yearly cost. Cancellation date reminders. Category grouping.

## 80. Price Point
Log prices of things you buy frequently. See price trends over time. Know if current price is good. Alert when price drops.

## 81. Wind Down
Evening routine checklist with customizable items. Track completion over time. Suggested routine based on sleep goals. Gentle reminder times.

## 82. Morning Kick
Morning routine checklist. Time-box each item. Track completion time trends. Optimize routine based on data.

## 83. Focus Room
Create themed focus sessions: deep work, creative, administrative. Timer with session type. Distraction log (what pulled your attention). Weekly session summary.

## 84. Task Tunnel
Single task at a time focus mode. Timer running. No other tasks visible. Complete or abandon options. Session history.

## 85. Break Bingo
Micro-break activities shown during breaks. Complete activities for bingo. Random selection or choose from list. Track break quality.

## 86. Done Log
Log completed tasks throughout day. No organizing, just capturing. View by date. Weekly productivity word cloud from task names.

## 87. Delegation List
Tasks that could be delegated or outsourced. Time estimate for each. Cost estimate if outsourcing. Status tracking (to delegate, delegated, done).

## 88. Waiting On
Track things you're waiting on from others. Follow-up reminder dates. Status updates. Escalation suggestions for old items.

## 89. Someday Maybe
Ideas to do someday, not now. Tag by category. Move to active list when ready. Periodic review reminders.

## 90. Project Outline
Lightweight project planning. Major milestones with dates. Task list per milestone. Progress percentage. No complex dependencies.

## 91. Habit Pair
Pair habits together: after I do X, I will do Y. Track both habits. See if pairing increases consistency. Switch pairings to optimize.

## 92. Trigger Track
Identify habit triggers. What preceded the habit? Time of day? Location? Mood? Build awareness to change habits.

## 93. Tiny Win
Log small wins throughout day. Build collection of positive moments. Review when feeling down. Weekly win summary.

## 94. Feel Better
Log actions that improved how you feel. Mood before/after. Identify patterns in what helps. Build personal wellness toolkit.

## 95. Network Log
Track people you meet. Where, when, what you discussed. Follow-up reminders. Notes on interests for future conversations.

## 96. Learn Link
Save learning resources with tags. Mark as to watch, in progress, completed. Notes on key takeaways. Review cycle for spaced repetition.

## 97. Vocab Grow
New vocabulary you're learning. Word, definition, example. Review mode with spaced repetition. Pronunciation notes.

## 98. Concept Map
Visual diagram of interconnected concepts. Add concept nodes and relationships. Color code by category. Export as image.

## 99. Quiz Me
Create self-quizzes from your notes. Multiple choice or fill-in-blank. Score tracking. Weak areas highlighted for review.

## 100. Progress Page
Personal dashboard of metrics you care about. Mix of productivity, health, and life metrics. Quick glance at overall wellbeing. Customizable widgets.
